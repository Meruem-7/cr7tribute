CRISTIANO RONALDO

Important life stamps of Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo Dos Santos Aveiro was born in a poor family, his father suffured from alcohol addiction. His mother wanted to abort him due to poverty and for already having 3 children.


At age 15, he was diagnosed with tachycardia, a condition that could have forced him to give up playing football.


From age 16 he played for Sporting CP youth team & from 2002 to 2003 he played for Sporting senior team.


During the 2002-03 season, his representatives suggested him to Liverpool & Barcelona. Arsenal manager Arsene Wenger was also interested in him & met him to discuss a possible transfer.


In august 2003 Sporting defeated Manchester United by 3-1 & United's manager Sir. Alex Ferguson was impressed by him and wanted to sign him on a loan deal for 1 year, but the United players wanted him to be signed permanently. After the game, Ferguson agreed to pay Sporting £12.24 million to sign Cristiano.


At United he requested the number 28, his number at Sporting, he received the squad number 7 shirt, which had previously been worn by great United players as George Best, Eric Cantona and Davclass Beckham.


In 2008 Cristiano became United's first Ballon d'Or winner since Best in 1968, and the first Premier League player to be named the FIFA World Player of the Year.


In 2009, Ronaldo joined Real Madrclass for a world record transfer fee at the time, of £80 million. At least 80,000 fans attended his presentation at the Santiago Bernabéu, surpassing the 25-year record of 75,000 fans who had welcomed Diego Maradona at Napoli.


He played for Real Madrclass till 2018, by then he won 4 more Ballon d'or & 5 Champions's League trophies with real Madrclass. He's also the highest Real Madrclass goalscorer of all time.


He signed for Juventus in 2018 and went went on to win 2 consecutive Serie A titles and scored 100+ goals for juventus until he left the club in 2021 to return to Manchester United again.


His club career overshadowed his international career until he won Euro 2016 with Portugal.


Cristiano Ronaldo is the highest goalscorer in world football for both club (811) & country (115).


He is often touted as the greatest football player of all time and one of the most influential athletes in the world.
